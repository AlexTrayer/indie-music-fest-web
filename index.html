What is this?

Independent music festivals provide essential platforms for emerging artists and offer a unique, community-focused experience distinct from mainstream commercial events. Often curated by music enthusiasts rather than large corporations, these festivals prioritize creative integrity and diverse lineups over mass appeal. They serve as crucibles for new trends and provide a space for music fans to discover niche genres and underground talent in intimate, often scenic, settings.

Highlights from previous years

Iggy Pop's Legendary Set (2017)

The "Godfather of Punk" delivered one of the most iconic performances in the festival's history, a moment organizers Phil Pirrone and Phil Margolis cite as a top highlight. He returned for the festival's 10th anniversary in 2022

The 2018 Storm

A major storm cut Tame Impala's headlining set short, a moment that became part of festival lore. The band made good on a promise to return, playing their album Lonerism in full at the 2022 event.

"Khru-Tang" Collaboration (2019)

A surprise and instantly iconic collaboration saw the psychedelic trio Khruangbin close out the festival with members of the rap legends Wu-Tang Clan.

Moving Locations

The festival moved from Sunset Ranch Oasis in Mecca to the Institute of Mentalphysics in Joshua Tree in 2016, and then to Lake Perris, each location adding a unique desert/lakeside vibe to the experience.
