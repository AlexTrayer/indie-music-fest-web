Festival Lineup

This year's Dazed Music Festival lineup promises a diverse and electrifying mix of emerging talent and established indie favorites. Headlining the main stage is the critically acclaimed psychedelic rock band "The Midnight Bloom," known for their atmospheric soundscapes and captivating light shows. Supporting acts include the high-energy post-punk band "Commuter" from Athens, the soulful funk fusion of "Groove Nation," and a special late-night electronic set from rising star DJ "Synthwave Dream," ensuring a genre-spanning experience for all attendees.

Featured genres at the 2025 festival

  1. Psychedelic Rock: Expect swirling guitars, atmospheric soundscapes, and hypnotic rhythms from acts like The Midnight Bloom and Desert Vibes.
  2. Post-Punk: High-energy performances with driving basslines and sharp vocals, exemplified by Athens-based band Commuter.
  3. Funk & Soul Fusion: Infectious grooves and powerful vocals from groups such as Groove Nation.
  4. Electronic/Synthwave: Late-night beats and retro-futuristic soundscapes perfect for dancing, primarily from DJ Synthwave Dream.
  5. Indie Pop: Catchy melodies and thoughtful lyrics from a few of our smaller stage acts.
